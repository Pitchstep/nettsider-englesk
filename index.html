Culture & People

English-speaking Canadians are known for their friendliness, multiculturalism, and love of the outdoors. The culture blends British, French, Indigenous, and global influences. Hockey, music, and festivals are central to life here.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: Late spring to early fall.
  • Currency: Canadian Dollar (CAD).
  • Language: English (French in some regions).
  • Transport: Extensive public transit in cities, good intercity connections.
  • Respect local customs and Indigenous lands.
